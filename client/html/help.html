FAQ

1、Main purpose of this app

This app helps z Systems administrators easily access the maintenance information that they need to keep their z Systems environment stable and healthy. The IBM z Service Advisor is a smart IBM z software solution that can reduce the amount of time needed to optimize Db2 for z/OS and CICS software maintenance. You can use the IBM z Service Advisor app to search for and evaluate which service to apply. Urgent service information, such HIPERs, PEs, and Red Alerts for DB2 and CICS, can be pushed to the mobile client, where you can evaluate the potential impact and access recommendations to help you in the decision process.

2、Searching

Click the Search icon and enter the PTF number or a keyword to quickly find the information you need.

3、Customize

Click the filter icon on the main page to display the Preferences view, and enter the keywords that you are interested in to receive information about only the PTFs that you care about.

4、WishList

You can bookmark PTFs for further review by clicking the bookmark icon on the top right corner of the PTF Summary/Details view. You can access bookmarked PTFs by clicking the Wish List icon.

5、Feedback

To provide feedback about this app, open the Account view and click Feedback.
